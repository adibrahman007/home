Research

My research is on the theory of the evolution of binary stars with a particular focus on double white dwarf mergers. I also work on common-envelope evolution.

My work involves the computation of stellar-evolution models, using 1D codes – MESA star and the Cambridge descendant of the Eggleton evolution code STARS/BS07 – and the rapid evolution code SSE/BSE.
